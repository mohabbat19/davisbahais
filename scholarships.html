4Annual Allocation
‚ñº

The LSA will normally include in its annual budget an amount designated for scholarships.

  • The individual's need for assistance
  • The number of requests received
  • The total funds available

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply if I'm not a Bah√°'√≠?

Yes! The policy applies to any member of the Davis community who wishes to attend an event that advances the aims and principles of the Bah√°'√≠ Faith or builds capacity for service, community-building, or social action.

How much financial assistance can I receive?

There is no fixed amount. The Assembly determines awards based on the nature of the event, your demonstrated need, available funds, and the number of requests received.

What if I miss the six-week deadline?

You can still apply, but late applications may receive reduced funding or no funding, depending on the availability of resources after timely requests are considered.

Do I need to contribute any of my own money?

Yes. Applicants are expected to contribute toward the cost of the event through personal means, family support, or fundraising. This demonstrates commitment and allows the Assembly to assist more people.

What happens after I submit my application?

The Assembly will review all timely requests together and strive to notify you of their decision within two weeks of the application deadline.

Ready to Apply?

If you need assistance to attend a qualifying event, we encourage you to apply early.
