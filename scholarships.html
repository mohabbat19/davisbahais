4Annual Allocation

The LSA will normally include in its annual budget an amount designated for scholarships.

There is no fixed cap per person or per event; awards are determined by the Assembly based on:

  • The extent to which the event advances the aims and principles of the Faith
  • The individual's need for assistance
  • The number of requests received
  • The total funds available
