Notes to the Feast Facilitator

Progressive Themes Throughout the Year:

Feasts 1-3 (Foundation): Establish understanding of America's destiny and the society-building power of the Faith. Help friends grasp the vision of what we're building together.

Feasts 4-6 (Capacity Building): Deepen appreciation for the institute process and youth empowerment. Build capacity for systematic learning and reflection.

Feasts 7-9 (Action & Service): Explore the core activities and social action. Connect spiritual principles to practical service.

Feasts 10-12 (Community Development): Focus on cluster movement, racial unity, and creating spaces of spiritual refuge in neighborhoods.

Feasts 13-15 (Institutional Strength): Build understanding of the role of institutions and the importance of administrative capacity.

Feasts 16-19 (Vision Forward): Inspire with the vision of divine civilization, renew commitment to living sacrifice, and cultivate hope amid crisis.

Facilitation Tips:

  • Allow time for silent reflection before discussion
  • Encourage all voices to be heard
  • Connect themes to local cluster activities
  • Consider breaking into smaller groups for deeper discussion
  • Document insights and action points for follow-up
